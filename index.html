üéØ Design Principles for Global Credibility

  • Clean, medical-grade aesthetic: Light backgrounds (white/light blue), strong sans-serif typography, ample whitespace
  • Trust signals everywhere: Security badges, partner logos, credentials, data privacy certifications
  • Large buttons and text (many users 50+)
  • Audience segmentation on homepage: "I'm worried about my memory" / "I'm a caregiver" / "I'm a professional"
  • Sticky CTAs: "Take Brain Health Check" button visible on every page
  • Mobile-first design

‚ö†Ô∏è Critical Safety Requirements

  • Disclaimers on every tool page: "This is a screening tool, not a medical diagnosis. Please consult your doctor."
  • Data security badges: SSL, HIPAA-compliant (if handling health data), ISO certifications
  • Emergency resources: Always visible crisis hotline, suicide prevention numbers
  • Source citations: Every claim backed by research with links
